Ledgr - Battlehack SF
Ledgr

Ledgr is currently an application that helps users rent items to other user. It can keep track, process payments using Venmo, and send reminders to the owner and renter of the item that the rental is due soon.

Ledgr solves the problem of people owning items they don't use very often and others needing an item temporarily but don't want to purchase it. Owners can even rent their items out to others at a lower cost than a rental shop.

Owners earn money, while renters save money, encouraging everyone to create a green and social sharing eco-system.

HackResume
HackResume

HackResume allows applicants to upload their resume, social network links, and basic information to their mobile device. Once uploaded, recruiters are able to retrieve their information using a QR code. The recruiter is able to download the applicant's resume and information.

The recruiter's tablet app provides a user-friendly interface to write some notes about the applicant. Once he or she is finish, the applicant's data and notes are uploaded to the server for future reference/review.

The recruiter is able to search through all of the applications in their collection using filters by skills, positions applied for, and rating. The search results filter through all the data for the most qualified candidate.
